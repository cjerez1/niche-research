## F2 — TITLE FORMULA BANK

### Source channel's title templates (decoded)

Five formulas observed on Dr. Franklin's top performers:

1. **Authority warning + medication callout**
   "Beware! [N] Popular Medications That Can Trigger [Severe Outcome]"
   Example: "Beware! 9 Popular Medications That Can Trigger Rapid Dementia"

2. **Substitution hack**
   "Don't [Common Habit] — THIS 1 Trick [Benefit]"
   Example: "Don't Drink Plain Water — THIS 1 Trick Boosts Blood Flow Fast!"

3. **Authority refusal**
   "[N] [Things] I'd NEVER [Do] After [Age] — Doctor Explains"
   Example: "5 Medical Checkups I'd NEVER Get After 65 — Doctor Explains"

4. **Symptom decoder**
   "If [Body Signal], Your Body Is [Surprising Cause]"
   Example: "If Your Ears Are RINGING All the Time, Your Body Is Lacking THIS!"

5. **Mortality hook**
   "He Died From Doing This! I Beg You, Don't Do It!"
   Variant of: "He Died in His Sleep From Doing This! I Beg You, Don't Do It!"

### Banned phrases (do not use — too close to source)

### Title rules

- 8–14 words ideal
- ALL CAPS used ≤3 words per title (avoid TikTok-feel)
- Numbers in arabic form (5, not five)
- No emojis
- Em-dash separator allowed (matches source style)
