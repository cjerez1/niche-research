## NOTES

- Keep weekly checks under 30 minutes total. If a tracked channel takes more than 5 minutes, simplify the data points.
- The Winning Titles and Winning Thumbnails banks are the highest-value outputs of this tracker — they feed MUSE for daily ideation.
- Drop channels from the list aggressively. A watchlist of 5 active competitors beats 15 stale ones.
- If a tracked channel pivots niches, decide within 7 days whether to keep tracking or drop.
